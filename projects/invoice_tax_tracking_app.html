Custom Invoice and Tax Tracking App

A mobile-friendly AppSheet system designed to help a small business create invoices, manage line items, and track tax-related sales data.

Overview

This project focused on building a practical invoicing system for a small business that needed a cleaner way to handle customers, products, invoice line items, and tax tracking. The goal was not to create an overly complicated accounting platform. The goal was to give the owner a simple system that worked better than manual tracking.

The app was designed around real business use: quick invoice entry, organized product selection, tax-exempt handling, and structured data that could support future reporting.

The Problem

The business needed a better way to create invoices and track tax-related totals. Manual systems can work for a while, but they become messy when products, customers, tax-exempt sales, and invoice details start piling up.

The biggest issues were:

  • Invoice data needed to stay organized
  • Product selection needed to be simple
  • Line items needed to connect properly to invoices
  • Tax-exempt customers needed to be handled correctly
  • Tax reporting needed to be easier to review later

The Solution

I designed an AppSheet app connected to structured Google Sheets tables. The system separated invoices, invoice line items, customers, products, and tax data so the app could work more like a real business tool instead of one giant spreadsheet. The app was planned so a non-technical owner could use it without needing to understand the backend structure.

What I Built

  • Customer table
  • Invoice table
  • Invoice line item table
  • Product category structure
  • Product and item selection workflow
  • Quantity and pricing fields
  • Tax-exempt handling
  • Tax-related invoice totals
  • Tax run and reporting structure

Tools Used

AppSheet Google Sheets Spreadsheet Data Modeling Relational Table Structure Workflow Design Tax Reporting Logic

Business Value

The app gives the business a cleaner way to create invoices and keep tax-related information organized. It reduces the need to manually piece together totals later and creates a more reliable structure for tracking sales, tax-exempt sales, and taxes collected.
